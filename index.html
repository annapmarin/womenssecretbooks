What is Women's Secret Books?

Women's Secret Books is specialist in sending boxes of books written by women to its customers. You can customize your boxes by choosing from different genres, themes and author preferences. Each box includes a selection of surprising and exciting books written by women from all over the world.

In addition to offering an exciting and diverse reading experience, we also aim to support women authors and promote sex equity in the publishing industry. By buying a box, customers not only discover new and exciting books written by women, but also support these authors and help increase visibility for their works.

The monthly box is a great option for people who enjoy reading and want to discover new books written by women. It's an easy and fun way to expand your literary horizons and support women authors at the same time.

Why reading women?

Reading female authors is important for several reasons. First, it can help promote equity and women visibility in the publishing industry. Often, the works of female authors are undervalued and have less visibility compared to those of male authors. By reading female authors, you can support them and help increase visibility of their works, which can contribute to greater representation of women in the publishing industry.

Second, reading female authors can help expand our literary horizons and have a more diverse and representative perspective. Women have had different experiences and perspectives than men, and this is reflected in their writing. By reading female authors, you can gain a deeper understanding of life and the world. Additionally, the works of female authors often tackle themes and issues that are important to women and are often ignored or underrepresented in literature.
